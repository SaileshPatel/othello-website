Welcome

Welcome to our developmental website for Team 4's implementation of Othello.

Who?

We are a team of nine, with various skill sets and abilities, who have come together to build a fully functioning video game based on Othello, a popular board game.

If you'd like to find out more about the team, head over to our About page.

Why?

This is a development blog for our group project for the module CS2010. In our group project, we are re-creating the board game, Othello, as a video game.

Our intended purpose for this project is to create a 21st century version of Othello in order to revitalise a board game for a new generation.

What?

Othello is a strategy-based game centered around placing pieces on a board. The aim of the game is to flip the opponents pieces in order to have more pieces on the board.
